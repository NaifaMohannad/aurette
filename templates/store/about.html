Our Story

About Aurette

Est. 2020

Crafting Beauty,
One Piece at a Time

Aurette was born from a passion for fine jewellery and a desire to make luxury accessible. Founded in Dubai, we set out to create a brand that celebrates the beauty, strength, and elegance of women through timeless jewellery.

Every piece in our collection is carefully crafted by master artisans using the finest materials — from ethically sourced diamonds to pure 22K gold. We believe jewellery is not just an accessory; it's a story, a memory, and a legacy.

Today, Aurette serves thousands of customers across the Middle East and beyond, bringing the finest jewellery experience to women who deserve nothing but the best.
